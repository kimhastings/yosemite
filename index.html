Dogs and Yosemite

Yosemite is a great place to visit, but not so much so for dogs, no matter how well-mannered they are. Sadly, dogs are not allowed on trails in the national park (you can read more about Yosemite's pet policy here).

This map shows hikes near Yosemite, but outside the park boundary, where dogs are welcome. Happy hiking!
